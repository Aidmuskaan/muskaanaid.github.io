Our Beginning

Muskaan Aid is a newly forming NGO based in India, with a vision to support underprivileged children, especially young girls. Our organization is preparing to launch its first initiatives in June 2025.

We recognized that many young girls across India suffer silently from period pain without access to basic relief. At the same time, countless children lack proper school supplies. Muskaan Aid was born from the desire to address these fundamental needs with dignity and care.

Our Mission

Our focus areas will include:

  • Providing menstrual comfort through electric hot water pads
  • Distributing essential school supplies to underprivileged children
  • Conducting health and hygiene awareness programs
  • Organizing seasonal aid (winter clothes, rain gear, etc.)
  • Building a network of compassionate volunteers

Upcoming Initiative

First Distribution Drive: June 11, 2025

We're excited to announce our inaugural project - distributing 100 electric hot water bags to girls in government schools. This will help provide relief from menstrual pain and allow them to attend school comfortably.

We're currently identifying schools with the greatest need and preparing our distribution plan. Volunteers will be needed for:

  • Pre-distribution surveys
  • Packing and organizing supplies
  • On-ground distribution
  • Documentation and follow-up

Why This Matters

Across India, many young girls miss school days every month due to period pain. Simple solutions like hot water bags can make a dramatic difference in their education and future opportunities.

Similarly, countless bright students struggle without basic notebooks or proper uniforms. Your support can help break these barriers and give children the tools they need to succeed.
